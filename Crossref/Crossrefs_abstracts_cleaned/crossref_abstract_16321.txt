The purpose of this study is to investigate the relationship between retirement anxiety, resilience and jinx dependence, college golf player. To achieve the goal of this study, we collected total 214 survey from college golf player in Gyeonggi, Gangwon, Chungnam and Jeonnam. Using SPSS 18.0 and AMOS 18.0. We conducted frequency analysis, confirmatory factor analysis, reliability analysis, correlation analysis and structural equating modeling. The following results of the study are; 1) Retirement anxiety of college golf player had a negative effect on resilience. 2) Retirement of college golf player had a positive effect on jinx dependence. 3) Resilience of college golf player had no-effect on jinx dependence. Therefore, psychological strategies should be developed to improve the resilience of college golf player.
