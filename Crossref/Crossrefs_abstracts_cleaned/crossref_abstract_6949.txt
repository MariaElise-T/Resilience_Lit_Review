During and after the pandemic, digital transformation is the driving force behind organizations' business resilience. In addition, continuous innovation should be encouraged. Business resilience is a firm's capacity to forecast, plan, respond and adapt to change in order to survive. Business resilience must be supported by digital transformation, one of the breakthroughs that can jump-start an entire organization. The interrelated factors include dynamic capabilities, technological capabilities, agile leadership, innovative ambidexterity, and knowledge stock.
