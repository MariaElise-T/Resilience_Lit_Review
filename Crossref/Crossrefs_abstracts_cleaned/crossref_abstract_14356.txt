This is a complete book that fits the aims and scope of the SpringerBriefs on Case Studies of Sustainable Development. This book offers a multidisciplinary perspective on resilience in the context of islands. By exploring different facets of resilience, from social inclusion to wayfinding,
 the authors aim to provide a comprehensive understanding of what resilience means for island communities, and how it can be fostered in the face of crises like the COVID-19 pandemic.
