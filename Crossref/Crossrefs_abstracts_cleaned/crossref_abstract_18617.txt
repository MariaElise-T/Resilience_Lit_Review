Artificial Intelligence (AI) has made significant global impacts across various domains. However, it is evident that certain areas have yet to harness the full spectrum of opportunities AI can provide. This review aims to investigate the transformative effects of AI on diverse sustainable goals, including the development of resilient infrastructure, the promotion of inclusivity, and the cultivation of innovation. By shedding light on previously unnoticed challenges within the realms of industrialization and infrastructure, this study unveils a novel perspective on the potential for an AI-driven industrial and innovative world while preserving and enhancing efficiency
