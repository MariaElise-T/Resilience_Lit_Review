The study aims to identify the contribution of ZISWAF to the availability of capital, management, and marketing of MSMEs’ products. This study also looks at the development and role of ZISWAF and MSMEs during the COVID-19 pandemic in Indonesia. Indeed, the COVID-19 pandemic has affected the economic condition due to the implementation of large-scale social restrictions. This study used a qualitative approach with the library method.
