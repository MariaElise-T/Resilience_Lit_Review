In order to cope with economic globalization and informatization and the increasing risks of global economy, the resilience of industrial chain has become a hot topic. This paper summarizes the existing industrial chain, toughness and relevant domestic and foreign literature on industrial chain toughness, analyzes the connotation and characteristics of industrial chain toughness, summarizes the measurement method of industrial chain toughness, and refines the path to improve industrial chain toughness. Therefore, the paper analyzes the problems existing in the study of industry chain toughness and provides some references for future research.
