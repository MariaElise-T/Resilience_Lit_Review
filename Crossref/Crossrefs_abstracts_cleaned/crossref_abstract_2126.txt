In 2006 the New York times, there were many articles about the struggles that were happening in Somalia and Ethiopia due to climate change. At this time 300,000 Algerians lost their lives to terror this plays into climate change due to the common feature of extremist groups being born as a byproduct of popular frustration in this case it was resource scarcity. At that time we didn't think much of these issues and did not link these issues back to climate change. This edition of Poplar & Ivy will explore the impact of climate change and strategies for resilience against it.
