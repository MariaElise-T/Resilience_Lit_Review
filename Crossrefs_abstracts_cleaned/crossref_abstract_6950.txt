Business resilience in digital technology era is not comprehensively overviewed. Thus, it is important to construct the body of the research and contributes to the current state of knowledge to understand the definition of business resilience in disruptive era. This study employs a systematic LITERATURE REVIEW of 76 papers that are categorized into two stream propositions: resilience and adaptive capability in digital technology era, and non-adaptive resilient one. The result reveals that when the business process adapt to digital technology, become more resilient and competitively sustained, while other do not. Future research may focus on speed adaptation capability in connectivity era.
