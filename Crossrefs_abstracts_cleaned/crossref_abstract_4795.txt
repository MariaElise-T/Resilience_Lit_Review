This summary provides an overview of a two-year project in secondary schools, creating group work to meet the needs of very different groups of youngsters with the aim of fostering resiliency. Secondary Group Work (SGW) is for professionals who recognise the importance of and who wish to promote Emotional Literacy skills and resilience. The paper raises awareness of the usefulness of group work as well as the important role of schools in taking forward a resilience led approach. The authors also propose a working model for professionals to consider when setting up, carrying out and evaluating such work.
