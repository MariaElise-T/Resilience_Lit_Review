Abstract
This article intends to bring to the attention of specialists and the general public alike contemporary educational methods to be applied in training for the awareness and internalization of values specific to the field of societal resilience, with critical infrastructures as the central framework of approach, with their main characteristics related to the fragility of the state of security. The article has a concrete practical value due to its applicability at the level of a centrally located county in Romania. The main infrastructures with critical value for determining potential disruptive events are mapped using specialized software, followed by the presentation of some institutions necessary to be involved for implementation of table-top exercises and, in conjunction or separately, field study trips. As a central point of the research methodology, recent literature highlights are emphasized, which robustly materialize and contextualize the practical approaches presented.
