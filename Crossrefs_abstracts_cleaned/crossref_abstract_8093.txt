This study aims to determine the role of high-performance work systems on employee engagement and employee resilience and also the effect of employee engagement on employee resilience. Literature studies are based on 23 international journals and data are collected using search engine methods. The results showed that high-performance work systems have a positive effect on employee engagement and employee resilience and employee resilience plays an important role in supporting this influence.
