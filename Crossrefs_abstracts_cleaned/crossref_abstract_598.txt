Objective: This study was conducted aimed to evaluate the relationship between resiliency and instability of marriage in married students of Islamic Azad University of Abhar. Methodology: The study was correlational and random sampling was carried out. The studied sample in this study were including 200 students. In this study, Conner and Davidson Resilience Scale (CD-RISC) and questionnaire of the instability rate of marriage was used. To analyze the data, Pearson correlation test and F-test was used. Findings: The results showed that there is a significant inverse relationship between resiliency and instability of marriage. Conclusion: These findings can be useful in the area of preventive interventions.
