Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) affects many healthcare providers who worked during the COVID-19 pandemic. Identifying the symptoms, precipitating factors, and available treatments is essential to mitigate long term effects on personal, patient, and organizational outcomes. PTSD may lead to chronic health conditions, poor patient care, and contribute to the nursing shortage.
