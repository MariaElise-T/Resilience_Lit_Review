Key TakeawaysFor many communities across the United States, shifting trends in climate and other environmental conditions are threatening water supplies.The pressure is on water providers to adapt—to explore alternative supply sources and develop resilience strategies for the long term.Aspen, Colo., enhanced the reliability of its water supply and closed the gap between supply and demand with integrated water planning.Aspen created and evaluated several options for its 50‐year adaptive plan, considering climate impacts on supply and demand as well as other potential threats.
